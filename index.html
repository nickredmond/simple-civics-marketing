As a citizen, compete with other members of your community to see who can have the biggest drive toward change and positive impact. Leaders of the community will enjoy a spot on the public leaderboard, as well as receiving points that can be exchanged for sweet prizes.

As a community organizer, share your upcoming demonstrations, rallies, volunteer opportunities, elections, meetings, and other community-oriented events. Your information will be shared with everyone in a centralized forum that is easy to access, so your rates of attendance and involvement may improve with no end in sight.
