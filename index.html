How does it work?

1. Community organizations post new events.

1. Community organizations post new events.

Organizers of charity associations, social activism groups, and governments can quickly enter information about upcoming events. Once submitted, the events will instantly become visible to the whole community, and users in the area will have opportunity to browse and find events with ease, instead of needing to know which organizations have upcoming opporunities for civic engagement.

2. Users search for events.

2. Users search for events.

Today, many people who are interested in being involved in the community have to browse countless websites, advertisements, and bulletins before finding the right opportunity. By comparison, Simple Civics gives users the power to effortlessly search and discover new opportunities that fit the criteria they're seeking. Community engagement has never been easier at a time we need it most.
